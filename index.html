Welcome to the Math and Science Club at Gahr High School

Our club is all about exploring the exciting worlds of math and science. We meet to work on fun projects, solve problems, and learn new things together. Whether you love numbers, experiments, or just want to learn more, you'll find something interesting here. Come join us and discover how math and science can be fun!

Who

The Math and Science Club at Gahr High School is open to all students interested in exploring math and science.

What

We engage in various activities including experiments, projects, competitions, and discussions to enhance our understanding of math and science.

Where

Our meetings are held in Room 205 at Gahr High School.

When

We meet every Wednesday after school from 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

Why

Our goal is to foster a love for math and science, develop critical thinking skills, and provide a platform for students to collaborate and learn.

How

You can join the club by attending one of our meetings or signing up at the club fair. All are welcome!

Math Events

We participate in math competitions and host math-related events throughout the year.

Science Events

We conduct science experiments and projects, and take part in science fairs and competitions.

Events

Stay tuned for our upcoming events where you can engage in exciting math and science activities.

Community

We aim to build a supportive community of students who share a passion for math and science.

Learn

Our club provides resources and opportunities to enhance your knowledge and skills in math and science.

Join

Interested in joining? Come to our next meeting or contact us for more information.
